PRIVACY POLICY

LAST UPDATED: APRIL 19, 2026
Argus ("we", "our", "us") is committed to transparency and protecting user privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how it is handled.

1. OVERVIEW

Argus is a global intelligence and data visualization platform. We do not knowingly harvest, sell, or exploit personal data. All user-related data is limited to what is necessary for authentication, platform functionality, and service improvement.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect only the minimum data required to operate the platform.

A. Account Information

When you create an account:

  • Email address
  • Authentication credentials (securely handled via backend services)

B. Usage Data

We may collect limited technical data such as:

  • Device/browser type
  • Session activity (for performance and debugging)
  • General interaction with features

This data is used strictly for improving platform stability and usability.

3. WHAT WE DO NOT DO

We explicitly do not:

  • Sell user data
  • Track users across unrelated websites
  • Build personal behavioral profiles for advertising
  • Intentionally harvest sensitive personal information
If any data beyond core functionality is ever introduced, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

4. HOW YOUR DATA IS HANDLED

All data is processed through secure backend infrastructure:

  • Authentication and database services are handled via Supabase
  • Backend logic and API handling are managed through Netlify Functions

We do not manually access or process user data outside of these systems unless required for debugging, security, or legal compliance.

5. PAYMENTS

If you subscribe to a paid plan, payments are processed by Stripe.

  • We do not store your payment details
  • Payment information is handled directly by Stripe's secure infrastructure
  • We may store limited metadata (e.g., subscription status)

6. DATA RETENTION

We retain user data only as long as necessary to:

  • Maintain your account
  • Provide access to the platform
  • Comply with legal obligations

Users may request account deletion, which will remove associated data where feasible.

7. SECURITY

We implement reasonable safeguards through our infrastructure providers to protect data from:

  • Unauthorized access
  • Misuse
  • Disclosure
No system is completely secure. Users should take standard precautions to protect their accounts, including using strong passwords and not sharing credentials.

8. USER CONDUCT & ENFORCEMENT

To maintain platform integrity and user safety, the following activities are strictly prohibited:

  • Attempted data extraction or scraping
  • Unauthorized access or intrusion
  • DDoS or service disruption attempts
  • Doxing or targeted harassment
  • Any form of cyber-related criminal activity

Violations will result in:

  • Immediate and permanent account termination
  • IP/address-level bans where applicable
  • Potential reporting to relevant authorities

9. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

Argus relies on trusted third-party services to operate:

  • Supabase — authentication and database
  • Netlify — hosting and serverless infrastructure
  • Stripe — payments

These providers may process data as part of their services under their own privacy policies.

10. YOUR RIGHTS

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Withdraw consent where applicable

Requests can be made by contacting us directly.

11. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy as the platform evolves.

When changes are made:

  • The "Last updated" date will be revised
  • Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of the updated policy

12. CONTACT

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, you may contact us through the platform or official communication channels.

ARGUS Intelligence Platform
Email: privacy@argus-platform.com

13. TRANSPARENCY COMMITMENT

Argus is actively evolving. While the platform is not yet in a final state, we are committed to:

  • Minimizing data collection
  • Being transparent about how systems operate
  • Not knowingly engaging in exploitative data practices
If that ever changes, it will be clearly disclosed in an updated Privacy Policy.

By using ARGUS, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.
